# Modern Variety Adoption Timeline

Regional time-series of modern (high-yielding) variety adoption shares
and their effect on harvest index. Used to correct residue:product
ratios for historical periods when traditional varieties dominated.

## Format

A data frame with columns:

- region_HANPP:

  World region name (8 regions).

- Year:

  Year (decadal from 1900 to 2020).

- Modern_share:

  Share of area under modern varieties (0-1).

- HI_correction_factor:

  Multiplicative correction for residue:product ratio (\>1 = more
  residue in traditional systems).

## Source

Evenson & Gollin (2003) Science 300:758-762. Krausmann et al. (2013)
Global Env Change 23:1170-1181. Pingali (2012) PNAS 109:12302-12308.
